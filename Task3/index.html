About Me

software engineer & front-end developer

I'm a passionate software engineering student at the Faculty of Computers and Information, Zagazig University. I started my programming journey with C++ and object-oriented programming (OOP), applying these skills in projects and problem-solving exercises. I have completed my studies in Data Structures and Algorithms, and I am now focusing on enhancing my front-end development skills. I have already mastered HTML and CSS, completed multiple projects using them, and I am currently advancing in JavaScript, Bootstrap, and React.js to build more sophisticated applications. I am an active member of the CS Frontend team at the Zag Eng Family community, a member of the Frontend team at the GDG on Campus Zagazig, and a member of the HR team at Zag Eng Family. Additionally, I have successfully completed a 7-day Frontend Bootcamp with GDG on Campus Zagazig, which enriched my practical experience and deepened my understanding of frontend technologies. I also completed Level 0 training in the ICPC Zagazig Community, which strengthened my problem-solving skills and teamwork experience. With a strong drive to learn and grow, I am always seeking opportunities to improve and contribute to the field of software development.
